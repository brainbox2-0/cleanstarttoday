Our Tax Relief Services

Offer in Compromise

Settle your tax debt for less than you owe. Our experts negotiate with the IRS to get you the best possible settlement.

Penalty Abatement

Get penalties removed from your tax debt. We help qualify you for first-time penalty abatement and reasonable cause relief.

Payment Plans

Set up affordable monthly payment plans with the IRS. We negotiate terms that fit your budget and stop collections.

Tax Lien Relief

Remove federal tax liens from your property. We help get liens withdrawn, released, or subordinated.

Currently Not Collectible

Get temporary relief from IRS collections. We help qualify you for Currently Not Collectible status when you can't pay.

Back Tax Preparation

Get caught up on unfiled tax returns. Our CPAs prepare accurate returns to minimize penalties and interest.

About Clean Start Today

Your Trusted Tax Resolution Partner

Founded in 2005, Clean Start Today has helped thousands of individuals and businesses resolve their tax problems with the IRS and state tax agencies. Our team of Enrolled Agents, CPAs, and tax attorneys bring decades of experience in tax resolution.

We believe everyone deserves a fresh financial start. Our mission is to provide expert, ethical tax relief services that remove the stress of tax debt and put our clients on the path to financial freedom.
